Our Belief

Space is not
a container.
It is a condition.

We began Forma XIII with a single provocation: that most designed spaces ask nothing of the people who inhabit them. They are comfortable, competent, and completely forgettable. We wanted to make the opposite — spaces that change how you feel the moment you cross the threshold. Spaces that ask something of you in return.

This is not a philosophy of difficulty. It is a philosophy of depth. We believe that a room, like a poem, should reward attention. That the light at four in the afternoon should be different from the light at nine in the morning, and that both should be considered. That the temperature of a stone under the palm of a hand is as important as the floor plan.

We work slowly. We take fewer projects than we are offered. And we give every one of them the same thing we would want given to us: our complete and undivided attention.

Our Practice

We listen
before we
draw.

Forma XIII was founded in Cairo in 2020, at a moment when the world had paused and space itself had become a subject of urgent conversation. We asked: what does it mean to be at home? What does a room owe the person inside it?

Our studio operates across architecture and interior design, residential and commercial, with work in Cairo, the Gulf, and the Levant. We are small by intention — eight people who have chosen depth over scale, and who believe that the relationship between a studio and its clients is one of the most intimate collaborations a person can enter into.

Every project begins with a long conversation. We visit the site alone, before we bring drawings. We try to understand not just what a client wants to build, but what they want to feel. Then, and only then, do we begin.

What We Stand For

Our Values.

01
Precision

Every decision is deliberate. We work slowly, thoroughly, and without compromise on what matters.

02
Honesty

We use materials for what they are — never to simulate something they are not.

03
Permanence

We design for decades, not seasons. Our spaces are meant to deepen with time.

04
Listening

The client's life is the brief. We spend more time understanding than designing.

05
Silence

We believe restraint is the highest form of design intelligence. Silence is a material too.

06
Craft

We work only with makers who share our standards, ensuring every detail is resolved in the making.
